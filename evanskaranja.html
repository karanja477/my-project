Descripition of the Project

The project is a web-application that generates a name of a Ghananian child based on the date he/she was born and their gender. It provides a form where an individual or the user should input their birthdate and choose their gender. Dates should follow a particular format as will be explained in the subsequent paragraphs. Non adherence to the date format will result to a snap. The project has adopted several technologies to ease the workability of the application.

Key in your Birth Details

Enter your birth date in the format as decribed here. mm/dd/yy. where
  1. Where "mm" stands for the month one was born"
  2. Where "dd" stands for the day one was born"
  3. Finally, "yy" relates to the year of birth

Choose your gender

At the second stage, choose the gender as either Male or Female as project in the field. It is imperative to provide the date in order for the web-application to generate the output. Otherwise, the web-appilcation will not respond as expected. The same goes in handy with the appropriate and accurate date and in lign with aforementioned format.

Submit

This is the the final stage of interacting with the project. Once gender and birt dates have been input, the user has to click the submit button. A pop up button with the quotes, Your name is ....
